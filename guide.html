01
What is Swarm Intelligence?
A classroom tool for anonymous personality profiling and peer feedback.
For participants
Complete a Big Five personality assessment on your phone. Then review anonymous profiles of your peers and give feedback — all without knowing who is who.
For the host
Create and manage the session. Control when the feedback round starts — everyone reviews each other.
The Big Five dimensions
O Openness C Conscientiousness E Extraversion A Agreeableness N Neuroticism
02
Two Roles
The app has two distinct flows depending on your role.
Host
Creates & manages the session
Generates a session code, monitors incoming signals, and controls when the feedback round starts.
Participant
Assesses & gives peer feedback
Completes the personality assessment, explores the five dimensions, then reviews anonymous peer profiles and gives each other feedback.
03
Host Flow
Running a Session
Step-by-step guide for the host.
1
Tap "Host a swarm session"
A unique 5-character session code is generated. Share it with the group.
2
Monitor incoming signals
Watch as participants join. You'll see signal count and ready status — no data is shown yet.
3
Start swarm feedback
Once enough participants are ready, tap "Start swarm feedback". Everyone now reviews anonymous profiles together.
4
Review signals together
Navigate through anonymous profiles one by one. Everyone sees the scores and can discuss and add notes.
5
Finish review
Notes are sent to participants. Everyone can now see their own scores alongside the feedback they received.
04
Participant Flow
Joining a Session
Step-by-step guide for participants.
1
Tap "Join a swarm"
Enter the 5-character code shared by the host.
2
Complete the assessment
Answer 20 questions (4 per dimension). Each has 4 options — pick what feels most natural.
3
Signal transmitted
Your anonymous profile joins the swarm. You won't see your own scores yet.
4
Dive into your dimensions
Learn about each Big Five dimension through interactive cards and the fish school visualization.
5
Signal ready & give peer feedback
Tap "Ready for swarm feedback". Once the round starts, everyone reviews anonymous profiles together and gives each other feedback.
6
View your pattern
After the feedback round, your scores are revealed along with the feedback you received from your peers.

07
Privacy & Design
Built for trust and psychological safety.
Anonymous by design
No names, no accounts, no login. Profiles are labeled "Signal #01", "#02", etc. Only the participant sees their own scores.
No data persistence
Session data lives in Firebase during the session. Each participant writes only to their own path — no race conditions.
Scores hidden until feedback
Participants don't see their own scores until the feedback round is complete. This prevents bias during peer review.
Ocean theme
Deep-sea aesthetic with bioluminescent particles, caustic light, and rare ocean creatures. Calm, fluid, alive.
